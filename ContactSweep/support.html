Support

Need help with ContactSweep, have feedback, or want to ask a privacy question? Send a message and we will help as soon as possible.

Contact Email

For support, bug reports, feature requests, and privacy questions, email:

contact@99haqh.com

What to Include

To help us understand your request faster, please include:

  • A short description of the issue or question
  • Your device model and iOS version, if relevant
  • The ContactSweep app version, if available
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if you are reporting a bug

Privacy Questions

ContactSweep only accesses your contacts to find duplicate contacts and does not store or send your contact list anywhere. For more details, please read the Privacy Policy.

Response Time

We aim to respond to support messages as soon as possible. Response times may vary depending on message volume and the detail needed to investigate your request.
