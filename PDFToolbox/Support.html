Support

Need help with PDF Toolbox, want to report a bug, or have a privacy question? Send a message and I will take a look.

Contact Email

For support, bug reports, feature requests, feedback, and privacy questions, email:

contact@99haqh.com

What to Include

To help understand your request faster, please include:

  • A short description of the issue or question
  • Your device model and iOS or iPadOS version, if relevant
  • The PDF Toolbox app version, if available
  • The tool you were using, such as Merge PDFs, Compress PDF, Scan to PDF, or Add Signature
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if you are reporting a bug

Camera Access

PDF Toolbox uses camera access only for the Scan to PDF feature. You can change or revoke camera permission at any time in your device settings.

Privacy Questions

PDF Toolbox processes its core PDF tools locally on your device and does not collect or share your documents. For more details, please read the Privacy Policy.

Response Time

I aim to respond to support messages as soon as possible. Response times may vary depending on message volume and the detail needed to investigate your request.
