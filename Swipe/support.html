Contact

For support, questions, bug reports, or feedback, email contact@99haqh.com.

What to include

When reporting an issue, please include a short description of what happened and what you expected to happen.

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The sWipe app version
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible

Photo access

sWipe needs photo library access to show photos for review and request deletion of photos you choose to remove. Your photos are not uploaded to servers operated by sWipe.
